Two layers will be used, the Physical and the Application.

Application Layer deals with 
	- Displaying of text
	- Displaying of graphics
	- Button interfaces, menu items
	- Keyboard Input being sent to the read/write functions
	- Establishing serial connection settings
	- Providing user help
	
	Functions dealing with this layer
		- WinMain()
		- WndProc()
		- checkMenu()
		
Physical Layer deals with
	- Intiating the serial connection
	- Reading from the serial connection
	- Writing to the serial connection
	- Terminating the connection
	
	Functions dealing with this layer
		- init()
		- execRead()
		- writeOutputBuffer()
		- endSession()
